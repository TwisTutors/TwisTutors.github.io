General Questions

Is tutoring free? How much does it cost?
Tutoring is absolutely free. We understand that not everyone is in a place to spend money on tutoring, so we've assembled the best in the business to give you free, high-quality tutoring.
How do I become a tutor?
If you're interesting in becoming a tutor, please fill out the interest form.
What subjects do you tutor in?
We can tutor in any subject necessary. We have a team of dedicated tutors proficient in High School Mathematics, High School Science, High School English, and High School Social Studies. We also help tutor students for the SAT/ACT and AP Exams.
What qualifications do I need to become a tutor?
It depends on which subjects you would like to tutor. If you fill out the interest form, we will contact you with more information.
